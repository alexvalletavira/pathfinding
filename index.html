Welcome to my Pathfinding Visualizer!
This is a short explanation on how this works:
Select an specific pathfinding algorithm by selecting the "Algorithms" menu.
Paint the board with blue to add obstacles. You can switch to white paint to erase any specific obstacles you have painted.
To paint, click one on a square. You will paint any square you're hovering over until you click again.
You can drag and drop the Start and End nodes in any square you want.
The status text shows you if the algorithm is being displayed or not. You can only start a process if the previous one has finished.
You can use the "Clear Pathfinding" and "Clear Blocks" to clean the board of pathfinding squares and blocking squares respectively.
